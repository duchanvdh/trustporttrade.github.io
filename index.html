About Us

TrustPort Trade B.V. is a Rotterdam-based commodity trading company specializing in the sourcing and distribution of raw materials. We connect reliable suppliers from Asia, Africa, and the Middle East with buyers across Europe, ensuring secure transactions and long-term partnerships.

What sets us apart is our deep knowledge of international logistics and transport, enabling us to deliver not only materials but also peace of mind. Our focus is trading — with logistics expertise as our strength to guarantee smooth and efficient operations for every deal.

At TrustPort Trade, we stand for reliability, transparency, and growth — building bridges in the global raw materials market.

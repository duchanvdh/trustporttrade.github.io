About TrustPort Trade

At TrustPort Trade B.V., we connect European markets with trusted suppliers worldwide, with a strong focus on Indonesia. Leveraging our deep logistics expertise and certified by FIATA and FENEX, we ensure every shipment is handled with precision, reliability, and transparency. Our mission is to simplify global trade for our clients, offering agile, efficient, and secure solutions from sourcing to delivery.

What we value

  • Integrity & long-term relationships
  • Operational excellence in transport & logistics
  • Agility and smart automation as we scale
